## 2. NARRATIVE SUMMARY - BUSINESS OVERVIEW & STRATEGY

### Company Profile
**Deckers Outdoor Corporation** is a global leader in designing, marketing, and distributing innovative footwear, apparel, and accessories. The company operates through its iconic brands: UGG, HOKA, Teva, AHNU, Koolaburra, and UGGplush.

**Headquarters**: Goleta, California (250 Coromar Drive)
**Listing**: New York Stock Exchange (Ticker: DECK)
**Company Classification**: Large accelerated filer, well-known seasoned issuer

### Strategic Vision & Achievements

#### CEO's Statement (Stefano Caroti, CEO & President)
The company demonstrated exceptional performance in FY 2025, highlighted by:
- Record-breaking earnings per share (30% above prior year)
- Revenue reaching nearly $5 billion (16% YoY growth)
- Fifth consecutive year of double-digit growth
- Operating margin of 23.6% (200 basis point improvement)
- Effective marketplace management and brand strength

#### Long-Term Strategy
- **Consumer-First Approach**: Focus on products blending technology and comfort
- **Brand Building**: HOKA and UGG positioned as most in-demand and consumer-loved brands
- **Market Share Capture**: Both brands continuing to gain market share
- **Innovation Focus**: Relentless pursuit of disruptive product innovations

### Brand Positioning

- **Growth Trajectory**: Highest growth rate (24% in FY 2025)

## 3. OPERATIONS & BUSINESS PLAN

### Business Model Overview

#### Distribution Channels
1. **Direct-to-Consumer (DTC)**
   - E-commerce websites
   - Company-owned retail stores
   - Growing channel for brand control and profitability

2. **Wholesale Channel**
   - Specialty and department store retailers
   - Online marketplaces
   - Higher-end retail partnerships

3. **Partner Retail Program**
   - Third-party operated retail stores (primarily international)
   - Training and operational standard monitoring by Deckers

### Operational Strategy

#### Production & Supply Chain
- **Extended Design Cycle**: Months-long planning before consumer purchase
- **Manufacturing Decisions**: Long lead times requiring advanced forecasting
- **Pre-Season Orders**: Customers place orders up to 12 months in advance
- **In-Season Orders**: Immediate shipment capability for fill-in orders
- **Inventory Management**: Focus on SKU productivity and production lead time reduction
- **Liquidation Channels**: Excess inventory management through multiple outlets

#### Inventory Management
- **Production Cycle**: Design → Raw material purchase → Inventory accumulation → Sales → A/R collection
- **Key Focus Areas**:
  - Product planning and forecasting systems
  - Inventory control improvements
  - Supply chain management capabilities
  - Higher inventory turnover
  - SKU productivity discipline

#### Customer & Return Policies
- **Wholesale Returns**: Defective or improperly shipped merchandise
- **DTC Returns**: 30-90 days from point of sale (cash or credit)

### Retail Strategy

#### Direct Retail Operations
- Company-owned retail locations worldwide
- Focus on brand elevation and consumer connections
- Profitability management and underperforming store closures
- Geographic and market positioning optimization

#### International Expansion
- **Growth Strategy**: Transition certain brands between third-party and direct distribution models
- **Partner Retail Program**: Operating standards across all partner locations
- **New Market Development**: Expansion in emerging markets

### Supply Chain Infrastructure
- **Warehouse & Distribution Centers**: Company-operated and third-party logistics (3PLs)
- **Global Partnerships**: Third-party carriers and 3PLs
- **Manufacturing**: Overseas production with reliable supplier relationships
- **Supplier Requirements**: Ethical Supply Chain Supplier Code of Conduct compliance

### Key Operational Focus Areas

#### Product Planning & Forecasting
- Existing orders consideration
- Forecasted sales and budgets by brand
- Wholesale and DTC channel planning
- Customer delivery requirements integration

#### Technology & Systems
- E-commerce platform management
- Product planning and forecasting systems
- Inventory control systems
- Supply chain management capabilities

#### Sustainability & ESG Operations
- **Holistic ESG Program** (since 2010)
- Environmental impact minimization
- Sustainable business practices
- Multi-stakeholder initiatives with suppliers, employees, customers

#### Compliance & Governance
- **Supplier Code of Conduct**: Human rights, working conditions, anti-corruption, environmental compliance
- **ESG Oversight**: Corporate Responsibility, Sustainability & Governance Committee
- **Risk Management**: Audit & Risk Management Committee oversight
- **Board Oversight**: Regular updates on ESG program status

### Operational Challenges & Risk Management

#### Inventory & Working Capital Management
- Challenge: Extended production cycle with long lead times
- Strategy: Improved forecasting systems and inventory discipline
- Focus: Higher inventory turnover and SKU productivity

#### Competitive Operations
- Challenge: Intense marketplace competition
- Strategy: Agile marketplace operations and disciplined cost structure
- Focus: Continuous innovation and operational efficiency

#### International Operations
- Challenge: Managing multi-market, multi-channel distribution
- Strategy: Flexible transition between distribution models
- Focus: Maintaining brand standards across third-party partners

#### Talent & Organization
- Challenge: Attracting and retaining skilled talent
- Focus: Competitive compensation and benefits
- Location: Goleta, California headquarters
